About Movie4K

Movie4K is a modern, cross-platform movie streaming application built with Flutter. Our mission is to provide a seamless, user-friendly experience for movie enthusiasts across Android and iOS devices.

Developed with cutting-edge technologies including Firebase for real-time notifications, analytics, and cloud messaging, Movie4K brings you the best in movie streaming with intelligent features like watchlist management, personalized settings, and push notifications.
